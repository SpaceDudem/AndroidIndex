# Android Resource Collection

* Project address: [Github](https://github.com/getActivity/AndroidIndex)

* I have worked in Android development for many years and have collected many excellent websites and resources. Sometimes I thought about whether I could organize the valuable resources I’ve collected over the years and share them with everyone. This idea existed for half a year, and I finally completed it during the National Day holiday in 2022.  

  I compiled the websites frequently used in Android development and summarized them here. I hope they can help everyone. At the same time, everyone is welcome to submit [issues](https://github.com/getActivity/AndroidIndex/issues/new) to add resources or correct mistakes. After review and approval, I will add them to the project.  

  Building a good open-source project requires everyone’s contributions. Open source is a process of mutual assistance. Without everyone’s support it would be difficult for me to maintain it well.
